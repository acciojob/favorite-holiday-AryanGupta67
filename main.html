Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, romantic ambiance, and world-famous landmarks. It's a perfect blend of art, culture, and gastronomy.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home of the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A beautiful avenue known for shopping and cafes.
  5. Montmartre – A charming hilltop neighborhood with an artistic vibe.
  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes served with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Escargot – Snails cooked with garlic and parsley butter.
  • Ratatouille – A classic vegetable stew from Provence.
  • Macarons – Delicate, colorful meringue-based cookies.
